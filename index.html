About Wolframize

Wolframize is an app that will revolutionize how you learn math. See a problem that you don't understand? Just snap a photo of the problem with our easy-to-use Snapchat interface and our app will convert the photo to text using OCR Cloud 2.0, displaying the answer along with all the work needed to reach the answer through Wolfram Alpha! At the bottom of each problem, links will be provided to tutorials and other practice questions to further your understanding of the topic. Begin Wolframizing today!
